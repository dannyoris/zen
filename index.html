Zen Sushi uses high quality fresh fish imported by air from Japan every day. Nakamura-san personally selects the best types of fish according to the season for import. Zen also provides premium Wagyu grade beef. Our menu is customized to ensure that the best combinations are provided to our customers.

The restaurant interior was designed by European designers. Our restaurant features sushi bar seats where you can observe the exceptional skills of our chef as well as having friendly conversations with them. We also have private rooms if you prefer a little bit more privacy. Our rooms have flexible walls and can accommodate from 4 people up to about 20 people.

Our Executive Chef Nakamura Fumihiro-san is a native of Narita city of Chiba province, Japan. He has over 20 years of experiences in premium Japanese cuisine. He has worked in Tokyo, Singapore and Hong Kong. Nakamura-san’s restaurant in Hong Kong restaurant was featured in the renowned Michelin Guide. His restaurant in Tokyo was also frequented by celebrities from around the world. Nakamura-san specializes in making premium and creative Japanese sushi and delicacies.

Lunch
Our lunch menu mainly consists of Lunch Sets to cater for the customers in the business district. The Sets uses the same high quality fish and other fresh ingredients and the main courses are usually served with appetizer, soup, steamed egg, and desert.
Dinner
Our dinner menu offers a choice of A La Carte menu, as well as our renowned “Omakase”, meaning Chef Recommendation. Our creative A La Carte menu provides delicacies that are unique to our restaurants. Omakase is the traditional Japanese style “Chef Recommendation”, and Nakamura-san will choose the best fish and ingredients of the day and tailor-made a special course which varies from day to day.

Our restaurant also provides a good choice of premium Japanese Sake and Shochu, as well as premium Red and White wines, including Grand Cru class wines.

Come to Zen, and experience the truly authentic Japanese cuisine, freshly prepared by our renowned Executive Chef Nakamura Fujihiro.
